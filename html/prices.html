Women's clothing prices

The price list shows the initial prices for sewing a basic simple model. With an increase in the complexity
of the style, the number of decorative elements, and the size of 50, the cost of tailoring increases.
Therefore, the final price for the manufacture of the product is calculated individually in consultation
with the designer after clarifying all the details of the model. The prices are for tailoring only! Fabrics
and lining materials are not included in the indicated price. Sewing consultations are free!

The sewing period is from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the complexity of the style
and the current workload of the studio at the time of application!
